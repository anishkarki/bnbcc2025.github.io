Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our carpet cleaning services

How long does carpet cleaning take?

The time required depends on the size and condition of your carpets. Typically, cleaning a 3-bedroom home takes 2-3 hours. Drying time is usually 4-6 hours with our powerful extraction equipment.

Is your carpet cleaning safe for children and pets?

Yes, absolutely. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are completely safe for children, pets, and those with sensitivities or allergies.

Can you remove all types of stains?

We can remove most common stains including coffee, wine, pet stains, and more. However, some stains may have permanently damaged the carpet fibers. We'll always give you an honest assessment.

How often should I have my carpets cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every 12-18 months for residential carpets. For high-traffic areas or homes with pets and children, every 6-12 months is ideal.

Do I need to move furniture before cleaning?

We can move small furniture like chairs and tables. For larger items like beds and wardrobes, we recommend moving them beforehand or we can clean around them.

What's the difference between steam cleaning and dry cleaning?

Steam cleaning uses hot water extraction to deep clean carpets, removing dirt and allergens. Dry cleaning uses minimal moisture and is ideal for delicate fibers or when quick drying is essential.
