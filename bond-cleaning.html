Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bond cleaning services

Do you offer a bond back guarantee?

Yes, absolutely. This is our commitment that our work meets the high standards of property managers. If your agent is not satisfied with the clean and provides a report within 72 hours, we will return to rectify any issues free of charge.

Which suburbs do you service?

We provide bond cleaning services across all North Brisbane suburbs including Chermside, Ascot, Hamilton, Clayfield, Nundah, North Lakes, Sandgate, Redcliffe, and surrounding areas.

Are your products eco-friendly and safe?

Yes, we are committed to using effective, non-toxic cleaning agents that are free from hazardous chemicals. Our products are safe for your family, pets, and the environment.

What's the difference between a bond clean and a regular clean?

A bond clean is a comprehensive deep clean that follows a detailed checklist designed to meet real estate standards. It covers areas often missed in regular cleans, such as inside ovens, windows, and high dusting.

How long does a bond clean take?

The time required depends on the size and condition of your property. Typically, a bond clean for a 2-bedroom apartment takes 4-6 hours, while a 3-bedroom house may take 6-8 hours.

Do I need to be present during the clean?

No, you don't need to be present. Many of our clients prefer to provide access and return after the clean is complete. We'll send you photos of the finished job for your peace of mind.
