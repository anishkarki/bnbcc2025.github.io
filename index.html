Bond Clean vs. Regular Clean

A regular clean is for general upkeep. A bond clean is a far more comprehensive and intensive deep clean of the entire property, designed to meet the strict requirements of real estate agents for the return of your security bond. We follow a detailed, room-by-room checklist covering areas not typically included in a standard clean.
